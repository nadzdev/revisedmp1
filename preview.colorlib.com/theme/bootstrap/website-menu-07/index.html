About



Talisay Beach is located on the southwestern coast of Dinagat Island in the Philippines. It is a beautiful and secluded beach known for its white sand, clear waters, and stunning sunsets. The beach is not as developed as some of the more popular tourist destinations in the Philippines, which means it offers a more peaceful and relaxing experience. There are several resorts and accommodations in the area that offer various amenities and activities. You can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and diving in the crystal-clear waters or simply relax on the beach while soaking up the sun. The area is also known for its seafood, and you can try fresh catches at local restaurants.     HOW TO GET THERE

    To get to Talisay Beach in Dinagat Islands, you can follow these steps:

  1. Fly to Surigao City: The nearest airport to Dinagat Islands is Surigao City Airport. You can book a flight to Surigao City from Manila, Cebu, or Davao.
  2. Take a van or bus to San Jose: From Surigao City, take a van or bus to San Jose, the capital of Dinagat Islands. he travel time is approximately 3-4 hours.
  3. Hire a motorcycle or tricycle to Talisay Beach: From San Jose, you can hire a motorcycle or tricycle to take you to Talisay Beach. The travel time is approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour.


    4. Alternatively, you can also hire a private car or arrange for a tour package that includes transportation to and from Talisay Beach.
